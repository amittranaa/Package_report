14 Choose the Right Website for Your Business & Budget

Every business has different goals, timelines, and budgets. Below is our recommendation to help you choose the most suitable solution.

Option 1 – HTML, CSS & JavaScript Website

Recommended if:

  • You need a basic online presence.
  • Your budget is limited.
  • You don't require frequent content updates.
  • You only need a simple informational website.

Best For

Small businesses, Startups, Single-page or brochure websites

Estimated Investment

₹10,000 – ₹20,000

Not Recommended For:

Long-term SEO, Large service catalogs, Online booking systems, Future expansion

Option 2 – WordPress Website

Recommended if:

  • You need an affordable website with an admin panel.
  • You plan to publish blogs and update content regularly.
  • You want good SEO at a moderate budget.

Best For

Small & medium businesses, Clinics, Service-based companies

Estimated Investment

₹20,000 – ₹35,000

Not Ideal If:

You expect advanced healthcare modules, plan to build a patient portal or complex dashboards, or want a highly customized platform.

Option 3 – React.js Website

Recommended if:

  • You need a modern user interface.
  • Your focus is on custom dashboards or internal applications.
  • SEO is not the highest priority.

Best For

Dashboards, Internal management systems, Custom applications

Estimated Investment

₹30,000 – ₹45,000

Not Ideal If:

Your primary objective is to rank higher on Google.

⭐ Recommended

Option 4 – Next.js Website

Recommended if:

  • You want long-term business growth.
  • SEO is a priority.
  • You plan to invest in Google Ads and digital marketing.
  • You expect your website to grow with your business.

Best For

Diagnostic Centres, Hospitals, Healthcare Brands, Multi-location Businesses, Premium Providers

Estimated Investment

₹40,000 – ₹60,000

Advantages:

Excellent Google SEO, High website speed, Premium user experience, Better Google Ads performance, Future-ready architecture.

Supports AI chatbot, patient portal, online reports, dashboards, and additional integrations without rebuilding the website.
